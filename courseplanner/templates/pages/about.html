About CoursePlanner

CoursePlanner is a web application designed to help students plan and organize their courses with ease. Whether you're managing your current semester's courses or planning your entire degree, CoursePlanner provides the tools to keep everything organized.

Key Features

  • Track and manage your completed and upcoming courses.
  • Upload transcripts to automatically populate your courses.
  • Manual course input for adding custom courses.
  • Generate personalized course plans to stay on track for graduation.

Why Choose CoursePlanner?

  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Comprehensive course tracking
  • Secure and private course data
  • Customizable plans for every student's unique journey

CoursePlanner was built by JMU students Arthur Schevey, Joshua Nunez, and Cameron Harmon.
